Places to Eat in Charlottesville

Eating is one of the main tourist activities in Cville. First, check out this Washington Post article about all the great food.

Here's a rough guide to some of our favorite places to eat:

  • $ = about $3-8pp
  • $$ = about $9-14pp
  • $$$ = $20pp or more
  • C = near the Corner (by the Red Roof Inn and the University grounds)
  • D = Downtown (accessible by free trolley from Red Roof Inn or car)
  • W = on West Main, halfway between the Corner and the Downtown Mall
  • B = in the Belmont neighborhood (west of the Downtown Mall)
